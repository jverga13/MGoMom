Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

The best way to book is by filling out our online booking form. You can also call or text me at (313) 887-1856 for more immediate needs.

What are your hours of operation?

I am flexible and aim to meet your student's needs. While I primarily operate during standard business hours (8am to 6pm), I can accommodate evenings and weekends by appointment. For urgent matters, please call or text me directly at (313) 887-1856

What payment methods do you accept?

I primarly accept digital payments through platforms like Venmo and PayPal. Payment details will be provided and further discussed once a service is confirmed.

Is MGoMom a medical service?

No. While I can provide transportation to urgent care and deliver over-the-counter medications, MGoMom is not a medical service. **In case of a serious medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.**

What is your service area?

My services are centered around the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I cover the campus area and surrounding neighborhoods. If you have a request that is slightly outside this area, please contact me to see if I can accommodate it.

Can I request a service that isn't listed on the site?

Absolutely! The services listed are our most common requests, but I pride myself on providing personalized support. If you have a specific need, please don't hesitate to reach out and ask. I will do my best to help.
