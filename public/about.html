About Me

Hi, I'm B — the heart behind MGoMom. I'm a proud Michigan mom, a long-time Ann Arbor local, and someone who knows first-hand just how much it means to know your child is cared for, even when you're miles away.

I started MGoMom after experiencing the rollercoaster of sending my own child off to the University of Michigan. The adjustment is hard — not just for students, but for the parents who love them. Whether it’s a forgotten medicine, a rough day, or a stressful exam season, I realized there’s a real need for local, caring support that only a mom could offer.

Now, I provide practical help and warm reassurance to Wolverines and their families through services like care package deliveries, urgent care visits, move-in support, and even airport pickups. I'm here to make sure your student never feels alone, and you never feel helpless.

This work brings me joy — connecting with families, comforting students, and knowing that someone is close by when you can’t be. Whether it’s a text for help or a drop-off of soup and tissues, MGoMom is ready to step in.
