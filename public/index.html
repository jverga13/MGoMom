About Me

Hi, I’m B—the heart behind MGoMom. As a proud Michigan mom and long-time Ann Arbor local, I know how hard it is to have your child far from home. Whether it’s a forgotten medication, a tough exam day, or settling into a new dorm, I’m here to step in with practical help and motherly reassurance.

This work brings me joy—connecting with families, comforting students, and ensuring someone they trust is close by. From a text for help to a drop-off of soup and tissues, MGoMom is ready to step in.
